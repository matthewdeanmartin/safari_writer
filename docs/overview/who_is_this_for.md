# Who is Safari Writer For?

Safari Writer is for anyone who appreciates the simplicity and focus of early computing:

- **Retro Computing Enthusiasts**: Experience the feel of 8-bit word processing without the hardware limitations.
- **Distraction-Free Writers**: A minimalist, text-only interface that lets you focus on your words.
- **Keyboard Power Users**: Full control over your document through keyboard shortcuts and formatting markers.
- **Learners & Explorers**: Revisit the roots of modern word processors and see how things used to be done.

Whether you're writing a novel, a quick note, or just exploring the retro aesthetic, Safari Writer provides a unique and satisfying writing experience.
